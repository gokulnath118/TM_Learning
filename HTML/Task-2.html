Maggie

A quick, tasty, and satisfying meal that can be prepared in just a few minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Maggie noodles
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon oil (optional)
  • 1/2 cup vegetables (optional: peas, carrots, capsicum, etc.)
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder (optional for extra spice)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pan over medium heat.
  2. If youâ€™re adding vegetables, add them to the boiling water and cook for a couple of minutes.
  3. Add the Maggie noodles to the boiling water. Break them into smaller pieces if preferred.
  4. Stir the noodles well and add the seasoning that comes with the packet.
  5. Optional: Add red chili powder or other spices to enhance the flavor. Stir the noodles and let them cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Once the water is absorbed and the noodles are soft, add oil (optional) for extra flavor.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious, comforting plate of Maggie noodles!
